## How to Add a Finding

1. Copy [`_template.md`](_template.md) to a new file
2. Include: amount, period, entities, wards affected, status, evidence sources
3. Link to related [contractors](../contractors/index.md) or [wards](../wards/_template.md)
4. Cite RTI responses if applicable — upload to [rti-responses/](../rti-responses/_template.md)
5. Submit a PR

Every finding must have at least two independent sources or a government document.
