# KRIDL Section 4(g) Tender Exemption — Rs 4,700 Crore

## Summary

KRIDL, a government PSU blacklisted twice, received Rs 4,721 crore in BBMP contracts without any tender process using Section 4(g) of the KTPP Act. BNP's analysis of 63,629 BBMP projects found that nearly 50% of executed works worth Rs 10,018 crore went to this single entity. The Karnataka High Court stayed the practice in 2021.

## Details

- **Amount**: Rs 4,721 crore (BNP analysis); Rs 13,000 crore total (Deccan Herald, 2022); Rs 12,943 crore under ED scanner (2025)
- **Period**: 2015-2021 (active use of 4(g)); under ED probe for 10 years of works
- **Entity**: KRIDL (Karnataka Rural Infrastructure Development Limited) — Government PSU
- **Wards affected**: 198 wards (old BBMP boundary)
- **Status**: HC stayed 4(g) exemptions (2021); ED probe ongoing (2025); state attempted legislative amendment to restore loophole

## What citizens can do

- File RTI asking for list of all works awarded under Section 4(g) in your ward
- PIO address: Public Information Officer, GBA Zonal Office, [your zone], Bengaluru
- RTI fee: Rs 10 (by IPO/DD/Court fee stamp)
- Response deadline: 30 days
