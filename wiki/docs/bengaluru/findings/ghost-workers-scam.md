# Rs 934 Crore Ghost Sanitation Workers Scam

## Summary

BBMP paid salaries to 6,600 non-existent sanitation workers (pourakarmikas) for over 10 years. The scam was exposed by Joint Commissioner Sarfaraz Khan after a 1.5-year investigation. Biometric systems showed 30,000 registered workers vs 20,000 actual workers on the ground.

## Details

- **Amount**: Rs 934 crore (some reports say Rs 550 crore in fake bills specifically)
- **Period**: 2010-2020 (approximately)
- **Entities involved**: BBMP waste management contractors, BBMP officials
- **Wards affected**: All 198 BBMP wards (old boundary)
- **Status**: ACB raids conducted; ongoing investigation
- **Mechanism**:
  - Contractors held multiple ATM cards for ghost workers
  - Withdrew Rs 7 lakh monthly from ghost accounts
  - Paid actual workers only Rs 3,000-4,000 (mandated: Rs 7,000)
  - Rs 17,000/month per ghost worker = Rs 17 crore/month siphoned
- **Continuing (2025)**: 7 wards in Gandhinagar showing 3 shifts fraudulently, Rs 35 lakh/ward vs Rs 15 lakh normal. Rs 2.5 crore/month still being siphoned. Contractors are blood relatives of BBMP employees.

## What citizens can do

- File RTI to PIO, GBA Zonal Office asking for ward-wise pourakarmika count vs bills paid
- Attend ward committee meeting and ask for biometric attendance records
- Report ghost workers to Lokayukta: [lokayukta.kar.nic.in](https://lokayukta.kar.nic.in)
