# APRERA — AP Real Estate Regulatory Authority

## Portal

- **Main**: [rera.ap.gov.in/RERA/Views/](https://rera.ap.gov.in/RERA/Views/)

## What's accessible

- Searchable database of registered projects and agents
- Approved plans, land details, construction schedules, possession timelines
- Project status updates

## What kaun.city pulls

Phase 2: **APRERA-registered projects per ward** — when a Vizag user drops a pin, show registered real estate projects in the area with possession timelines and any complaints filed.
