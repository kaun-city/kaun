# Yo Vizag

Independent local journalism for Vizag — the closest equivalent to Citizen Matters or Deccan Herald's Bengaluru desk.

## Site

- **Main**: [yovizag.com](https://www.yovizag.com/)
- **About**: [yovizag.com/about](https://www.yovizag.com/about)

## Why this matters

Vizag does not currently have a dedicated civic journalism platform like Citizen Matters covers Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai. Yo Vizag is independent digital news with consistent civic coverage:

- GVMC zone reorganisations
- VMRDA Praja Darbar grievance program
- "Operation Lungs" coastal forest reclamation
- PGRS Spandana Monday tracking
- Civic project status

## What kaun.city pulls

City Pulse fact ticker pulls Yo Vizag's RSS feed daily, classifies headlines into civic categories (PUBLIC MONEY, ROAD SAFETY, ENVIRONMENT, ELECTED REPS, etc.), and surfaces the top items on the Vizag homepage.

This is the same RSS classifier that runs for Bengaluru with Deccan Herald, The News Minute, Citizen Matters. Adding Yo Vizag is one line in the feed list.
