# APEPDCL — AP Eastern Power Distribution

Vizag's power distribution utility. APEPDCL is HQ'd in Vizag.

## Portals

- **Main**: [apeasternpower.com](https://www.apeasternpower.com/)
- **Outage Management System**: [apspdcl.in/OMS/fdrCommStatus](https://apspdcl.in/OMS/fdrCommStatus?web=interLink)

## What's accessible

- **Feeder-level outage data** — which feeders are down right now, communication status
- Power supply analysis dashboards
- Bill enquiry, new connection status (per consumer)

The **feeder-level OMS** is the genuinely unusual public data — most distcos in India don't expose this. Lets a citizen see whether the outage is on their feeder, how many other consumers are affected, and how long.
