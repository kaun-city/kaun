# Open Budgets India — GVMC

Function-wise structured GVMC budget data — receipts and expenditure broken out the way a citizen can read.

## Portal

- **GVMC organisation page**: [openbudgetsindia.org/organization/greater-visakhapatanam-municipal-corporation](https://openbudgetsindia.org/organization/greater-visakhapatanam-municipal-corporation)
- **AP Finance Department**: [apfinance.gov.in/budget.html](https://apfinance.gov.in/budget.html)
- **AP Budget Portal**: [apbudget.apcfss.in](https://apbudget.apcfss.in/)

## Key numbers

- **GVMC 2024-25 budget**: Rs 5,457 crore
- **AP state 2025-26 expenditure**: Rs 2,97,929 crore

## What's accessible

Open Budgets India publishes GVMC's budget book in structured form:

- Function-wise receipts (property tax, water, trade licences, grants from state, capital receipts)
- Function-wise expenditure (revenue vs capital, by department)
- Multi-year time series for comparison

This is the "budget breakdown" view that citizens can actually parse — the GVMC PDF is dense and hard to navigate.

## What kaun.city pulls
