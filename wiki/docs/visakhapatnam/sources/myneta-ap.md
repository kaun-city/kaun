## What kaun.city pulls

Phase 1: **MLA card per ward** — name, party, criminal cases (if any), declared assets, education, profession. Same shape as Bengaluru.

The framing for Vizag is still factual reporting — what the MLA self-declared in their affidavit. Vizag's tone is "transparency" overall but EC affidavit data is presented identically for every city; we don't editorialise it differently.
